CONTRACT DIVISION

What we do

Step 1: Project Scope

Whenever you need to detail any kind of space or structure, a meeting with our designer shall be set up for you to lay down your vision.

Step 2: Site Visit

We will arrange to visit the location to better understand the area, space or item, and how the transformation will be approached.

Step 3: Design Presentation

Once all designing information for space or structure has been assembled, the designer is then tasked with providing a full 3d render of your project for visualization, correction and improvement purposes.

Step 4: Presentation

In reference to our client's schedule, the management creates a timeline within which contract signing, delivery and installations shall be done respectively.

Step 5: Installation and Delivery

Unispace fitters engage our clients during installation process and this also entails decision making relative to time and resources available to the task and vision in play.
