You will first be asked about the goals you have for your project. Once we have a clear understanding of these goals, with our broad knowledge on item selection, we will help you pick ones best suited to your needs. To ensure that you get the most out of our services at a price that works well with you, a timeframe and budget will be negotiated in your attendance

We will schedule a visit to your property so as to further discuss your idea and get a better understanding of the space and purpose you have in mind. During this visit, measurements of the space will be taken, also taking into account any locations of power outlets, door openings, hearths, skirting boards, etc.

After completing steps 1 and 2, we will have a clear understanding of the space you are working with and what you want to fill it with. This will enable us to use the measurements we have gathered to create a completely accurate representation in the form of a high-quality render showing how everything will appear when complete and in place.

Our third process allows you to make changes to the project as many times as necessary until you are completely pleased with how it appears in the renders and are certain you want to proceed with placing your order. After receiving your approval, we will order your items according to your specifications.

Your goods will be sent to our warehouse once you have scanned all of your options and placed your order. Our logistics´ staff will notify you when your goods arrive, and together schedule delivery and installation times. On the slated date, our qualified installers will show up and precisely install your products.
